Lesson Connection

The sport of lacrosse has created so many opportunities for me and I was fortunate enough to play for 4 years at Princeton University. I wanted to build an app to help youth and inexperienced players gain access to private lessons from some of the best lacrosse players in the country.

NYC Farmers' Markets

This project utilizes a D3 (Data-Driven Documents) map that shows the locations of farmers' markets in the New York City area in 2013. We implemented a jQuery UI slider to display circles on the map indicating farmers' markets that are open for service on that particular day.
